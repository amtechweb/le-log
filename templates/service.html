DELIVERY OF DANGEROUS CARGO

Our goal is to prevent and eliminate all possibile risks, which might have impact on people, cargo damage and enviorement. With our stricteness and precision, we make sure, that loading, transport and unloading are done as per rules. All of our drivers are trained and have the needed permission for handling ADR cargo. We make sure that everything is organzied, as per EU regulations.

DELIVERY OF PERISHABLE CARGO

We transport perishable cargo in reefer containers, with the required temperature, on a daily basis.

Our team makes sure that gensets are activated and set at the requested temperature. As soon as the containers are ready to load, we are already on the road, in order to minimize potential additional costs, such as storage, electricity, and D&D.
