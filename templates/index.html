ABOUT US

LE-LOG D.O.O. is a transport logistic company with broad experiences in container deliveries. Since the very beginning, in 2018, our goal was to establish long-lasting collaboration with our clients, by offering only the best: FAST, SAFE AND RELIABLE DELIVERIES.

Our highly skilled team and experienced drivers, are always ready to deliver your goods anywhere you need. We offer fast and on-time deliveries, with 24/7 assistance and regular updates about truck's position.

We want for our clients to feel supported and satisfied every step of the way, making sure that their cargo is in safe hands.

We are located near second entrance of Port of Koper.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of services and deliveries Europewide:
  • container deliveries
  • deliveries with standard tautliners
  • deliveries of ADR cargo
  • reefer deliveries
  • express deliveries
  • out-of-gauge deliveries

We can also provide seals for all types of containers.

In collaboration with our daughter company, we can support you also with:
  • customs procedures
  • port assistance
  • rail, air, and sea deliveries
  • pre-loadings and warehousing
  • inspections

OUR FLEET

Our fleet consists of 13 VOLVO and SCANIA trucks, with EURO 6 engines. Every truck is equipped with CVS tracking, which allows us to track the location at every step of delivery.

All of our trucks are well-maintained and all have ACTIVE GENSET for reefer delivery.

Due to increased demand, we are always investing in our fleet by purchasing new vehicles.
